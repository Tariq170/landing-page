Who is Jürgen Klopp?

Jürgen Klopp is a German football manager and former player. He is best known for managing Borussia Dortmund and Liverpool FC, winning major titles and thrilling fans around the world with his high-energy football and emotional leadership.

Top Achievements

Champions League

Won the UEFA Champions League with Liverpool in 2019.

Premier League

Broke Liverpool’s 30-year wait to win the English Premier League in 2020.

Bundesliga Success

Won back-to-back Bundesliga titles with Borussia Dortmund in 2011 and 2012.
